Problem: All devices offline in room
Condition: Gateway offline
Solution:
- Turn on circuit breaker
- If still offline, check AP indicator lights and cable connections
- If AP abnormal → Contact Superloop
- If AP normal → Contact Konec

---

Problem: Device offline
Solution:
- Turn on circuit breaker
- Restart device via circuit breaker
- Reconfigure device
- If still offline → Contact electrician

---

Problem: Gateway online but devices offline
Solution:
- Check AP status
- If AP issue → Contact Superloop
- Otherwise → Contact Konec

---

Problem: Lock cannot open
Solution:
- Remove all batteries and reinstall after 1 minute
- Check battery leakage
- Replace batteries if needed
- Reconfigure lock
- If still not working → Contact locksmith

---

Problem: Lock panel not lighting up
Solution:
- Connect to power bank
- If still no response → Contact locksmith

---

Problem: Lock panel not responding
Solution:

- Reconfigure lock
- If still not working → Contact locksmith

Problem: PIN or fob not working

- If still not working → Contact Konec
